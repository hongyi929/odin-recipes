Grilled Salmon

Description

Grilled Salmon is delicious yet simple.

One bite and you would feel like you've just been transported to heaven.

Ingredients

  • salmon fillet
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • lemon slices
  • garlic(optional)

Steps

  1. Brush the salmon with olive oil and season with salt and pepper
  2. Preheat the pan over high heat
  3. Place salmon skin side down and grill for 5-10 minutes
  4. Flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes until cooked through
