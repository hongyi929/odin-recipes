Porridge

Description

This yummy porridge is a staple food for many Singaporeans

It it commonly known as 'zhok' and paired with you tiao cut into pieces

Ingredients

  • rice
  • shallots
  • spring onion
  • you tiao
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Reduce heat and simmer until it thickens
  3. Add shallots, spring onions, you tiao and some salt for flavor
