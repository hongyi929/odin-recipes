Cupcake

Description

Cupcakes are small, cute and delectable!

They are the perfect thing to bake as a gift for friends and family.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • flour
  • butter
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius
  2. Mix butter and sugar in bowl until light and fluffy
  3. Add eggs, vanilla and milk and then stir in flour and baking powder
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean
