Description

Lasagna, famed worldwide, has ancient origins and remains an Italian superstar dish.

It is a baked casserole made with wide flat pasta and layered with fillings such as rag√∫, bechamel, vegetables and various cheeses.

Ingredients

  • ragu

Steps

  • Saute vegetables until soft
  • Spread a layer of ragu sauce on the bottom of a baking dish
  • Add a layer of lasagna noodles along with bechamel and vegetables
  • Repeat layers until the dish is full
  • Bake till golden and bubbling
